About Me

I am a final-year student at Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET), studying Materials and Metallurgical Engineering. With a CGPA of 3.26, I am passionate about material analysis and composite research.

Research & Thesis

Currently conducting my undergraduate thesis on polymer matrix composites using the hand lay-up method. My research focuses on hybrid composites with palm (taal) fruit fiber and coconut coir fiber as reinforcements and epoxy as the matrix. Experimenting with reinforcement levels of 5%, 10%, and 15% by weight, I am investigating the effects of these fibers on the composite properties.

Materials Analysis

Previously analyzed a chequer plate sample with the following composition: C- 0.183%, Cr-13.94%, Ni-1.48%, Si-0.486%, Mn-9.24%, Mo-0.0078%.
